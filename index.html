Rules
  • 1. The game has 2 players, playing in rounds
  • 2. In each turn, a player rolls a dice as many times as he whishes. Each result get added to his ROUND score. BUT, if the player rolls a 1, all his ROUND score gets lost.
  • 3. After that, it's the next player's turn
  • 4. The player can choose to 'Hold', which means that his ROUND score gets added to his GLBAL score.
  • 5. After that, it's the next player's turn
  • 6. The first player to reach 100 points on GLOBAL score wins the game
